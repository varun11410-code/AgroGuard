🤖 Gemini AI Summary

Late blight is a devastating oomycete disease caused by Phytophthora infestans. It spreads rapidly under cool, wet conditions and can destroy an entire field within days. Early intervention with appropriate fungicides and removal of infected plant material is critical to prevent significant crop losses.

💰 Budget Plan

Organic Treatment

Neem oil spray (0.5%), infected leaf removal, and improved air circulation. Apply every 7 days.

~₹200–400 per application
🌿 Standard Plan

Fungicide Spray

Mancozeb or Copper-based fungicide spray. Monitor weekly. Remove severely infected plants.

~₹600–1200 per application
⭐ Premium Plan

Integrated Management

Systemic fungicide + biocontrol agents + soil amendment. Full 30-day monitoring program.

~₹2500–5000 full program
